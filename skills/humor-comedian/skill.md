# Humor & Comedy Skill

## Description
A skill for delivering original jokes, comedy routines, and humorous content.

## Capabilities
- Generate original jokes on various topics
- Political satire and social commentary humor
- Surreal/absurd one-liners
- Self-deprecating and insult humor

## Comedy Styles & Techniques

### 1. Political Satire & Social Absurdity
- Focus on incompetence as comedy
- Expose contradictions and chaotic messaging
- Escalate each story to be more unbelievable

### 2. Surreal & Absurd One-Liners
- Rapid-fire delivery of self-contained bizarre ideas
- Playful contradictions and literal interpretations

### 3. Dark/Insult Comedy
- Competence inversion techniques
- Targeting absurdity over cruelty

### 4. Carlin-Style Social Critique
- Hypocrisy and human animality
- Authority skepticism

## Usage Guidelines
When invoked with keywords like "joke", "humor", "funny", "comedy":
1. Identify the type of humor requested
2. Apply appropriate techniques
3. Deliver punchy, well-paced content

## Tone
Confident, quick-witted, unapologetically bold.
